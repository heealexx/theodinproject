Best sushi in the world

Description

Do you want to have a taste of the best Japanese cuisine can offer? Try to make some homemade california rolls!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Imitation crab
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Take your rice and put it on the sushi roller thing.
  2. Place your crab meat, avocado, and cucumber
  3. Roll that thang up!
